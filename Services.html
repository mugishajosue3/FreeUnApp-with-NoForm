Our Services

Guidance That Changes Futures

Pre-Departure Support

Prepare for your new adventure with comprehensive pre-departure support Before you embark on your educational journey, we provide essential pre-departure support. From accommodation arrangements to travel guidance and tips for adapting to life in a new country, we ensure a smooth transition. You can leave for your destination with confidence, knowing that we have your back.

Scholarship Assistance

Unlock Financial Opportunities Education should be accessible to everyone, and FreeUnApp scholarships can make it a reality. Our scholarship assistance services we apply for you for various scholarship opportunities and our partners. We identify merit-based, need-based, and country-specific scholarships and other types of scholarship that can ease the financial burden of your education.

Study Abroad Consulting

Embark on a Journey of Excellence Our study abroad consulting services are tailored to help you choose the right educational path. Whether you’re pursuing a Bachelor’s, Master’s, or PhD program, our expert consultants are here to guide you. We assist you in selecting the perfect destination, university, and course that aligns with your ambitions. From understanding admission requirements to application assistance, we’ve got you covered.

Language and Test Preparations

Embark on a Journey of Excellence We offer comprehensive English language preparation tailored for international exams like IELTS, TOEFL, SAT, and GRE. Whether you're starting or just need a final boost, our personalized classes ensure you're fully ready for success. We also assist with the coaching for competitive exams, guiding you through every step — up to mastering question strategies and handling exam-day pressure with confidence.

Documentation Support

Embark on a Journey of Excellence We specialize in helping students craft compelling, scholarship-ready applications. From personal statements to recommendation letters, our team ensures your story is told powerfully and authentically — highlighting your strengths, potential, and goals. Beyond writing, we verify that every document meets global university and scholarship standards. Whether it's formatting, content, or completeness, we make sure nothing is left to chance — so your application stands out on an international level.

Career Counseling

We go beyond basic advice—our personalized counseling sessions uncover your strengths, passions, and long-term goals. Using proven career assessment tools and global industry insights, we match you with the right academic programs that set you up for success. With one-on-one mentoring, you’ll walk away with a clear, confident roadmap for your future.

Visa Assistance

From the moment you decide to study abroad, we handle your visa journey step-by-step. We prepare your documents to international standards, conduct mock visa interviews to boost your confidence, and keep you updated with the latest immigration rules so your application is accurate and stress-free.

Admission Guidance

We simplify the complex application process by helping you choose universities that align with your profile. Our experts polish your Statements of Purpose, essays, and recommendation letters to reflect your unique story. We ensure every requirement is met so your admission stands out in a competitive pool.

Information on Institutions

We provide in-depth profiles of top global universities—covering entry requirements, available scholarships, course structures, tuition fees, and campus life. Our curated database helps you compare and choose institutions that perfectly match your academic and personal preferences.

Travel Assistance

We make your travel smooth and worry-free. From finding the best flight deals and arranging travel insurance to giving you insider arrival tips, we ensure you’re ready to embark on your journey with complete peace of mind.

Accommodation Assistance

Finding the right home abroad can be overwhelming. We connect you with trusted housing providers offering safe, affordable, and comfortable living options near your university—matching your budget, lifestyle, and preferences.

Organizing On-Spot Admissions

Our exclusive events connect you directly with university representatives. Participate in interactive info sessions, submit applications on the spot, and secure conditional offers—often faster than traditional applications.
