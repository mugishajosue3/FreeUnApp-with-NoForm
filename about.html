Empowering Rwandan Dreams Through Strategic Scholarship Applications

At FreeUnApp, we believe that every Rwandan student deserves the opportunity to pursue international education, regardless of their financial background. We're not just consultants — we're dream-makers committed to your success.

  • Strategic Approach: We don't apply randomly. We research and target the top 5 institutions most likely to offer scholarships based on your profile.
  • Professional Application Process: From document review to essay enhancement, we ensure your application meets international standards.
  • Success-Based Pricing: You only pay when you succeed. Full scholarship (700K RWF) or partial scholarship (350K RWF) — no Advance Payments, no empty promises.
