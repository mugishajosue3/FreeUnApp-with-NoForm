Critical Requirement: You must ensure all submitted documents are authentic and valid:

Privacy Policy

Your Privacy, Our Priority

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with the best scholarship application services, we collect:

  • Personal Information: Name, contact details, date of birth, nationality
  • Academic Records: Transcripts, certificates, test scores, academic history
  • Application Materials: Essays, personal statements, recommendation letters
  • Financial Information: Only for payment processing when services are successful
  • Communication Records: Emails, messages, and application status updates

2. How We Protect Your Data

  • All personal information is encrypted and stored securely
  • Access to your data is limited to authorized FreeUnApp staff only
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information
  • Regular security audits and updates are conducted
  • Your documents are handled with the utmost confidentiality

3. Information Sharing

We will NEVER sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

  • Your information is shared ONLY with target institutions during the application process
  • We may share success stories (anonymized) for marketing purposes with your consent
  • Legal requirements may necessitate sharing information with authorities
  • We maintain strict confidentiality agreements with all our partners

4. Your Rights & Control

  • Access: You can request a copy of all information we hold about you
  • Correction: You can update or correct your information at any time
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your data after service completion
  • Consent: You can withdraw consent for marketing communications
  • Portability: You can request your data in a portable format

5. Data Retention

  • Application materials are retained for 3 years for reference and support
  • Success stories and testimonials are kept indefinitely (with consent)
  • Financial records are retained as required by law
  • You can request earlier deletion of your data
